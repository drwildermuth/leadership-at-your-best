Leadership at Your Best - Flashcard Journey

Context: Where did this experience happen? Was it in your personal or professional life?
Your Role: What was your role in this situation? Formal or informal leadership?
The Challenge: What was the main challenge you and your group were facing?
Your Actions: What did you do that showed you “leading at your best”? Be specific.
Connections to Assessments: Look at your results from the Personality Assessor, Leadership Practices Self-Assessment, and EQ 2.0. What strengths do you see reflected in your actions?
Lessons Learned: What are 3–5 key lessons you took from this experience?
Leadership Advice: Based on this experience, what advice would you give someone learning to lead?
